AI Concept • 2026-05-16

Phones warn at 10% battery. Humans don’t.

What if technology could estimate burnout, stress and emotional exhaustion before people completely crash?

The Problem

Phones, laptops and smartwatches constantly warn us when energy is running low. Humans don’t have that luxury.

Most people recognize burnout only after exhaustion, emotional overload, productivity collapse or complete mental fatigue.

The Concept

Human Battery → emotional energy tracking system.

The idea is not medical diagnosis. It’s behavioral estimation.

Possible Signals

Behavioral Data

  • • Sleep quality
  • • Screen time
  • • Notification overload
  • • Calendar stress

Cognitive Signals

  • • Context switching
  • • Typing patterns
  • • Social exhaustion
  • • Late-night activity

Why It Matters

The future of technology may not just optimize productivity. It may help humans understand their own mental and emotional energy better.
