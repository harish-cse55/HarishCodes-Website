Technical Breakdown

How SafeRoute TN could work behind the scenes.

SafeRoute TN explores a safety-first navigation system designed to prioritize safer routes instead of only fastest routes.

Safety Score Engine

Every road segment receives a dynamic safety score using signals like lighting conditions, crime density, crowd activity and historical incident reports.

Real-Time Crowd Signals

Anonymous movement patterns help estimate whether roads are isolated, active or high-risk during different times of day.

Route Optimization

Instead of optimizing only for time, the routing engine balances distance, safety score and environmental confidence.

Emergency Infrastructure

Nearby police stations, hospitals and emergency contacts can be integrated directly into the navigation flow.
